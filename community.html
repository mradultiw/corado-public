Community Standards

Who Corado Is For

Corado is a private network for verified corporate professionals.

Access is restricted to corporate email domains to:

  • Maintain signal quality
  • Reduce spam and public noise
  • Ensure accountability & build trust between members

Corado uses your corp email just to send an OTP for verification. It will never be used for promotional emails.

What Belongs Here

Corado is built for practical local needs, including:

Housing & flatmates
Buy / sell within community
Daily help & trusted services
Local advice & questions, Referrals

What Does NOT Belong

  • Influencer-style content
  • Spam or mass outreach
  • Political debates
  • Personal attacks
Corado is not social media.

Verification States

Blue Tick

If our system recognises your corporate domain, access will be granted immediately with a Blue Tick.

Clock Icon (Pending)

If your domain is not immediately recognized, your account is placed in a restricted state until we validate.

  • Limited posting/commenting quota
  • Temporary messaging limits

Why do we do this?

To prevent the community from email abuse, protect from spam, and maintain platform integrity. Once verified, normal access is restored. These restrictions are not personal — they are to maintain a trust environment.

Respect & Conduct

Be direct and respectful.

No harassment, discrimination, or doxxing.

Enforcement

To protect the community, Corado reserves the right to:

Remove posts Restrict accounts Suspend users
